About Steph Curry

little information

Youth

Born march 14,1988 to NBA player Dell Curry. As a scrawny kid, even while leading his high school team to three conference titles, he received no offers from major-conference schools. He decided to go to Davidson, a school that had not won an NCAA tournament since 1969.

College

Curry proved himself worthy, being the #2 scorer in the Southern Conference with 21.5ppg in his first year. In his second season, he increased this to 25.5 ppg leading Davidson to a 26-6 season, just losing in the conference finals. In his final year, Davidson again made it into the NCAA tournament, this time losing in the second round, finishing his last season with 28.6ppg.

Rookie

Curry was picked 7th overall in the 2007 NBA draft bij the Golden State Warriors. He averaged 17.5 points and 5.9 assists per game in his rookie season. He finished second in the rooky of the year voting and was named to the NBA All-Rookie First Team.

Currently

At the end of the 2010/2011 season Curry was out due to torn ligaments and had to get surgery. In the 2011/2012 season he only played 26 out of 82 games due to still recurring injuries. On the last day of the 2012/2013 season Curry set the record for most 3-pointers made in a single season. He would break his own record each new year since then and is on pace to break it again. They lost in the second round of the series to the Spurs. Next season, they didn't make it past the first round. After hiring a new coach, the warriors, leaded by a legendary MVP season of Curry, won the 2014/2015 playoffs.
